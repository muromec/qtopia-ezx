Supported Platforms

Qt is supported on a variety of 32-bit and 64-bit platforms, and can usually be built on each platform with GCC, a vendor-supplied compiler, or a third party compiler. Although Qt may be built on a range of platform-compiler combinations, only a subset of these are actively supported by Qt Software.

A more general overview of the platforms Qt runs on can be found on the Platform Notes page. Information about the compilers used on each platform can be found on the Compiler Notes page.
